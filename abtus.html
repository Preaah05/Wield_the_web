What we do

Flexfit's Gym Has Been The Authority In Fitness Since 1965 Dating Back To The Original Flexfit's Gym In Venice, California. It Was The Place For Serious Fitness.

In 1977, Flex's Gym Received International Attention When It Was Featured In The Movie Pumping Iron That Starred Arnold Schwarzenegger And Lou Ferrigno.

Flexfit's Gym Has Become The Preferred Gym Of Celebrities, Athletes, Bodybuilders, The Military, And Fitness Enthusiasts All Over The World. Most Importantly, Flexfit's Gym Continues To Change Lives By Helping People Achieve Their Individual Potential.
